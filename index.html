About Me

Hey, I’m Andrew Yu – product leader and engineer with a 10+ year career in tech, working in PM, engineering, design, and consulting roles. My specialization is in growth and AI products. Some of my previous work:

  • Built and redesigned the AI-driven discovery ecosystem @ LinkedIn.
  • Lead PM on the 2021 Inauguration putting on the most "ambitious, innovative inauguration ever" during COVID.
  • Worked on the 2020 election, edTech, enterprise B2B SaaS, ad tech, and non-profits.

I’m passionate about speaking and teaching. I’ve served as a coach for UC Berkley’s PM Studio program and instructor for Product School. I’m a lifelong classically trained multi-instrumentalist and artist.
